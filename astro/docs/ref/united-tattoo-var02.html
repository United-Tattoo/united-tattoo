Our Foundation

Three principles guide everything we do at United Tattoo

01

Family

You're not just a client here—you're family. We take time to know your story, your dreams, what makes you who you are. That connection shapes every piece we create together.

02

Community

Fountain raised us. This community shaped us. We're committed to giving back, supporting local artists, and creating a space where neighbors become friends.

03

Inclusivity

Your identity is valid. Your story matters. We welcome every body, every background, every journey. This is a judgment-free zone where you can be authentically you.
