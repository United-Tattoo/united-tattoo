Getting tattooed is easier when the basics are handled before you walk in. A little preparation helps your artist focus on the work and helps you settle into the session.

## Share the Details Early

Send your reference images, placement ideas, size range, and any style notes before your appointment. If something has changed since booking, let the studio know as soon as possible so your artist has time to adjust the plan.

## Eat and Hydrate

Have a real meal before your session and drink water throughout the day. Long appointments are easier when your body is not running on caffeine alone.

## Dress for the Placement

Choose clothing that gives your artist clean access to the tattoo area and can handle a little ink or stencil transfer. Layers are useful because studio temperatures can shift during a longer appointment.

## Bring What You Need

For longer sessions, bring a water bottle, headphones, and a small snack. Keep the setup simple so the workspace stays clear.

## Plan the Rest of the Day

Give yourself time after the appointment. Fresh tattoos need cleaning, careful clothing choices, and a quieter pace while your skin starts healing.

If you have questions about your appointment, reach out before the day of your session. Clear expectations make the whole process smoother.
