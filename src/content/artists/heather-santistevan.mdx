Heather Santistevan brings over a decade of creative experience to United Tattoo in Fountain, Colorado. Self-taught in the vibrant world of watercolors since 2012, she's built a style that's unmistakably hers—delicate fine line work layered with the kind of color that feels alive on the skin.

### Artistic Style

Heather's work lives at the intersection of watercolor and fine line artistry:
- Captivating watercolor designs with flowing, organic color
- Intricate fine line detail work
- Illustrative pieces that blend precision with expressive movement

Beyond the studio, Heather is an avid painter who channels her creative energy across multiple mediums. Her range shows in every tattoo—there's a painterly quality to her work that you won't find just anywhere.

### Philosophy

For Heather, art is more than aesthetics—it's a tool for healing. She's deeply committed to using creativity as a means to promote mental well-being, and that intention carries through every piece she creates. Whether it's a delicate floral or a bold watercolor sleeve, there's a warmth and intentionality in her process that clients feel from the first consultation.

> "Explore the fusion of skill, passion, and faith in every stroke."
