## About Pako

Pako Martinez is a vibrant force at United Tattoo, known for bold neo-traditional work and stunning use of color. His illustrative style brings a unique energy to every piece.

### Style & Approach

Pako's work is characterized by dynamic compositions, rich color palettes, and a playful yet refined aesthetic. He excels at transforming ideas into eye-catching, wearable art.
