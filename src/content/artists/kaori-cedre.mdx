Hey there—I'm Kaori, but you can call me Kei. I'm a tattoo artist at United Tattoo in Fountain, Colorado, and I'm still pinching myself that this is what I get to do for a living. I was always into art, and when my father started tattooing, I discovered a career that was everything I could dream of. He was my mentor, and I carry what he taught me into every piece I create.

### Artistic Style

I love working across styles, but my heart's in a few particular places:
- Illustrative work—especially statue designs with illustrative elements
- Black and grey realism with soft, intentional detail
- Script and lettering
- Funky animals (seriously, bring me your weirdest creature ideas)

I'm still fresh off the tattoo artist press, and honestly? I love that about where I am right now. I want to learn everything one possibly can about this field. Every new tattoo teaches me something, and that hunger to grow shows up in the work.

### Process

I take my time. My slow nature produces great work, and I'd rather spend the extra minutes getting something right than rush through it. When you come in, I'm going to ask about size, placement, style, whether you want color or black and grey, and when you're available. If you have references or images of what you want, send them over—I'll work with what you give me.

### Booking

Currently booking appointments and walk-ins are welcome. Sessions typically run 3-6 hours depending on the piece. I try not to go below $100, but if it's tiny and simple, we can work something out. For scheduled appointments, I like a $50-100 deposit to hold your spot.

> "I love what I do and am incredibly grateful for everyone who gets a tattoo."
