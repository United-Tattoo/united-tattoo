## About Amari

Amari Rodriguez brings a bold, vibrant approach to traditional and neo-traditional tattooing. With a foundation in fine art and illustration, their work blends classic American traditional motifs with contemporary color theory and composition.

### Style & Approach

Amari specializes in creating dynamic, eye-catching pieces that stand the test of time. Whether it's a classic sailor jerry design or a modern illustrative piece, each tattoo is crafted with precision and passion.
