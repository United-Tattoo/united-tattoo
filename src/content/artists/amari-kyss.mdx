Hey, you made it! Welcome. I'm Amari, a tattoo artist based out of United Tattoo in Fountain, Colorado. I specialize in American traditional: bold lines, saturated color, and timeless imagery that hits deep. I fell in love with this style because it's honest—it holds history, and it holds up on the skin and in the heart.

I actually started out in black & grey, and it still means a lot to me. It taught me how to slow down, how to see softness in the details, and how to work with intention. Even though traditional is where my soul lives now, I still carry that beginning with me and I love returning to it when it feels right for the piece.

### Artistic Style

My work centers around classic American traditional, but I love exploring different approaches:
- Bold lines and saturated color
- Timeless traditional imagery
- Black & grey with soft details and intention
- Rotating flash collection (check the portfolio!)
- Custom pieces that feel uniquely yours

Flash lovers, this one's for you—I've got a rotating collection of available flash designs ready to find a home. All flash is repeatable unless noted otherwise, and I'm always down to adjust sizing or color.

### Philosophy

Tattooing feels like a quiet kind of magic. It's deeply personal and bigger than just art—it's trust, storytelling, and shared space. My goal is always to make something that feels like you, while still staying true to my style. Something real. Something that'll last.
