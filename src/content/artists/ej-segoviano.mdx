## About EJ

EJ Segoviano is a resident artist at United Tattoo specializing in Chicano style, script work, and black and grey tattooing. His work honors the rich tradition of Chicano art while bringing a contemporary edge.

### Style & Approach

With deep roots in Chicano culture and artistry, EJ creates pieces that tell stories and carry meaning. His script work is renowned for its flowing elegance and perfect letterforms.

### Booking

EJ is currently accepting new clients. For script work, please include the text and any font preferences. For custom pieces, reference images are always helpful.
