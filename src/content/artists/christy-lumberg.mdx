## About Christy

Christy Lumberg is a resident artist at United Tattoo, specializing in fine line work, botanical designs, and geometric patterns. Her delicate approach to tattooing creates elegant, timeless pieces.

### Style & Approach

With a keen eye for detail and a steady hand, Christy excels at creating intricate designs that flow naturally with the body's contours. Her botanical work captures the beauty of nature in stunning detail.
