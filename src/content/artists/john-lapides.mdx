## About John

Meet the one and only John "Spray Day" Lapides. Although originating from Southern California, John has done his fair share of traveling the world learning from all sorts of cultures. Spending the last few years strengthening his craft in Colorado Springs, this is where he's really found his style! 

### Style & Approach

John has a well-rounded skill set, allowing him to thrive in almost any kind of work, but he enjoys and thrives with illustrated blackwork, geometric, floral tattoos and patchwork style sleeves. 

### Booking

As a featured artist in various tattoo magazines, and published by highly regarded news channels, John has over six years in experience under his belt. Crisp and clean fine lines mixed with striking stylized blackwork, John's work is truly distinguishable and more than ordinary.
