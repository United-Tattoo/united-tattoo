## About John

John Lapides brings decades of experience to United Tattoo, with mastery in both American traditional and Japanese styles. His bold lines and vibrant colors create tattoos that age beautifully.

### Style & Approach

John's work honors the classic traditions of tattooing while incorporating his own artistic vision. Whether it's a traditional eagle or a Japanese sleeve, each piece is executed with precision and respect for the craft.

### Booking

John is currently accepting new clients for both traditional and Japanese work. Large-scale projects and sleeves are welcome—please describe your vision in your booking request.
