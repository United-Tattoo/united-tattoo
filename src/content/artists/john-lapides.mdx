Meet the one and only John "Spray Day" Lapides. Although originating from Southern California, John has done his fair share of traveling the world learning from all sorts of cultures. Spending the last few years strengthening his craft at United Tattoo in Fountain, Colorado, this is where he's really found his style.

### Artistic Style

John has a well-rounded skill set, allowing him to thrive in almost any kind of work, but he particularly enjoys and excels with:
- Illustrated blackwork
- Geometric patterns
- Floral designs
- Patchwork style sleeves
- Traditional and Japanese work

His crisp and clean fine lines mixed with striking stylized blackwork make his work truly distinguishable and more than ordinary.

### Experience & Recognition

As a featured artist in various tattoo magazines and published by highly regarded news channels, John has over six years of experience under his belt. His dedication to the craft and unique artistic vision have earned him recognition across the tattoo community.
