Known professionally as "Sole," Steven Cedre brings vibrant energy and masterful technique to United Tattoo in Fountain, Colorado. With a distinctive signature style that merges photorealistic detail with bold cartoon aesthetics, Sole has carved out a unique niche in the tattoo world where technical precision meets playful creativity.

### Artistic Style

Sole specializes in bringing imagination to life through two powerful approaches:
- Color realism with rich, vibrant palettes
- Cartoon and animated character work
- Pop culture and media-inspired designs
- Dynamic compositions with bold visual impact
- Custom collaborative pieces

His work is characterized by crisp linework, stunning color saturation, and the ability to capture both the photographic realism of portraits and the exaggerated expressiveness of cartoon art. Whether you're looking to immortalize a beloved character or create a vivid, lifelike portrait, Sole's versatility ensures your vision comes to life with exceptional detail.

### Creative Philosophy

Sole approaches each tattoo as a unique collaboration between artist and client. His goal is to create pieces that not only look incredible on day one, but become cherished, permanent art that tells your story. With a keen eye for color theory and composition, Sole transforms skin into a canvas where realism and imagination coexist in perfect harmony.

> "Every tattoo is a chance to create something that's never been done before—something uniquely yours."
