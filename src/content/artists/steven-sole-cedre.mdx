## About Steven

Steven Sole Cedre is a resident artist at United Tattoo with a versatile approach to tattooing. From custom designs to realistic black and grey work, Steven brings technical excellence to every project.

### Style & Approach

Steven's work spans multiple styles, unified by his commitment to quality and attention to detail. He works closely with each client to bring their vision to life.
