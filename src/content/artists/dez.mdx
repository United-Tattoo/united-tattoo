## About Dez

Dez is a resident artist at United Tattoo bringing a unique perspective to blackwork and dark art. Their bold, striking designs make powerful statements on skin.

### Style & Approach

Specializing in high-contrast blackwork and illustrative dark art, Dez creates pieces that are both visually impactful and deeply personal. Each design is crafted to complement the client's vision and body.
