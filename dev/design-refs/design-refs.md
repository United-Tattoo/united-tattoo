# Design Guidelines for United Tattoo

A brief description of the provided example sites in the provided`.html` files.

## `site-1.html` 

**Where it matches:**

- has a strong layout that fits the overall layout aesthetics of United Tattoo
- minimal navigation and nav are appealing

**Where it doesnt match**

- Color palette doesn't fit the United Tattoo brand
- the terminal/hacker style doesn't fit with the tattoo brand ideology.

## `site-2.html`

This is not as strong of an example, but it's another example for variety. Ilike everything about it, but it's just not as strong as other examples. Use for reference when looking to build specific new components. 

## `site-4.html`

A strong example stylistically, probably the closest so far to the United Tattoo Brand identity. I think a combination between this and site-1 would go a long way in capturing the United Tattoo identity.

## `site-5.html`

This is a great example of micro interactions and animations, should definitely be used as reference.
